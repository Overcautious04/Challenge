Roronoa Zoro, a master swordsman, dreams of becoming the world's greatest. Trained rigorously from a young age, he vowed to fulfill this dream after the tragic death of his childhood friend, Kuina. Roronoa Zoro, a master swordsman, dreams of becoming the world's greatest. After the tragic death of his friend Kuina, he adopts a unique three-sword style and trains relentlessly. His life changes when he joins Monkey D. Luffy and the Straw Hat Pirates. Zoro's loyalty to Luffy is unwavering, demonstrated by his iconic sacrifice during the Thriller Bark arc. He constantly pushes his limits to surpass Dracule Mihawk, the current greatest swordsman. Zoro's story is one of determination, loyalty, and the relentless pursuit of greatness.
